# Company Reflection

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
I think Focus Bear was created to help people improve their focus and build better daily habits. From the induction video, I also noted that it was originally built for the team themselves, especially since the founder and many team members are neurodivergent. This makes it feel more real as it comes from their own real experiences with focus and routine.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Focus Bear is mainly solving the problem of lack of focus and consistency. Many people get distracted easily and find it hard to follow a routine especially with things like social media and lots of notifications.

## Why do you think this mission is important?
This mission is important because focus and consistency are key for both personal and professional growth. Without them, it becomes difficult to complete tasks, manage time properly and achieve goals.

## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
It aligns with my interests because I am currently studying and also doing an internship, so managing time and staying focused is very important for me. I am also trying to improve my productivity.

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes, I do relate to these challenges. There are times when I get distracted while studying or working, especially with my phone or by switching between tasks. Staying consistent every day is something I am still working on.

## Do you know someone who struggles with these challenges? How might Focus Bear help them?
One of my friends often struggles to focus, especially when studying or working on tasks for a long time. I didn’t think much about it earlier because he hasn’t been diagnosed with ADHD, but now I feel like he might be facing similar challenges. I think Focus Bear could help him by reducing distractions and helping him build consistent habits over time.
