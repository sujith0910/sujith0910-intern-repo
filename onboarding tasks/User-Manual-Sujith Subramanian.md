# How I Work Best

## I do my best work when...

Requirements are unclear or when there are too many context switches between tasks in a short time.

Clear, respectful, and reasonably detailed so I can fully understand the task or issue.

Discord or direct messages for quick communication. For detailed discussions, scheduled calls work better.

## If you need something urgent, do this...
Send me a direct message and clearly mention that it is urgent.

# Focus & Productivity

## I stay focused by...
Using structured to-do lists, breaking work into smaller tasks, and working in distraction-free sessions.

## Things that distract me...
Frequent interruptions and switching between too many unrelated tasks.

## The best way to support my productivity is...
Providing clear priorities and allowing enough uninterrupted time for focused work.
